Life Transformation, Rehabilitation & Restoration Center

The advent of the Covid-19 global pandemic brought about in its wake an aftermath of untold anguish, anxiety, deaths, downsizing of well to do firms and businesses, together with unprecedented job redundancies as most of the said business organizations vehemently struggled to stay afloat. One of the adverse effects of the nefarious pandemic is that untold numbers lost their respective jobs and some businesses went under, consequently multitudes of previously working classes were rendered jobless and were desperate to survive. Even affording to put a single meal on the table became a daunting task. Due to job shortages and businesses struggling to survive, many found themselves doing what they would ever do, which is prostitution or better put commercial sex in order to earn a living Even though this type of “Commercial” economic activity had never previously crossed the minds of these fine ladies, they were left with no other viable option other than to engage in commercial sex working in a very desperate attempt to make ends meet and provide basic needs for themselves and for their beloved children. The resultant effect of this, is that there was an overwhelming numbers of commercial sex workers that were simply pushed into it due to the prevailing tough economic situation that issued from the Covid-19 Pandemic. Many if not multitudes of these commercial sex workers were formerly very decent ladies that if given an alternative that would economically sustain them, they would be more than glad to abandon this illicit indiginifying trade.

LIFETRARECEN , which stands for Life Transformation, Rehabilitation, and Restoration Center, is dedicated to addressing the moral pandemic affecting many women who have not been able to regain their jobs or businesses.

Our Vision

To be the leading Non Governmental Organization that Positively Transforms,Restores and Rehabilitates Potentially Gifted Commercial Sex workers to Dignified livelihoods.

Our Mission

To meticulously identify Great potential and latent Talent in Commercial Sex workers, that can be harnessed, developed and empowered into sustainable,dignified livelihoods.

What do we do?

LIFETRARECEN seeks facilitate the recovery, rehabilitation and subsequent Restoration into decent livelihoods through:

  • Small business initiatives/Startups.
  • Income earning projects.
  • Educational support programmes.
  • Vocational trainings.
  • Minimal House rent & food up keep.
  • Mentorship programmes.
  • Life skills Trainings.
  • Counseling/Therapy.
  • HIV/AIDS checkups & Assistance.
  • Drug abuse rehabilitation, transformation and restoration.

How do we do it?

With the support of well-wishers-Individual, Foundations, Organizations both local and global , we seek to reach out these commercial sex workers that are more than willing to abandon this trade upon the availability of other economic sustenance activities. We will seek to meticulously identify these types of commercial sex workers that after thorough screening and proven satisfaction place them under the assistance of LIFE TRARECEN.
